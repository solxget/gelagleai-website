Privacy Policy for ገላግሌ AI Chat

Last updated: May 12, 2025

Introduction

Thank you for choosing ገላግሌ AI Chat. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience when using our application. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding your information and how we handle it.

No Data Collection Policy

ገላግሌ AI Chat does not collect or store any user data. We have designed our application with privacy as a fundamental principle.

  • We do not collect personal information
  • We do not track your usage or behavior
  • We do not store your chat history or queries
  • We do not use analytics tools to monitor user activity
  • We do not create user profiles or identifiers

How the App Works Without Data Collection

When you use ገላግሌ AI Chat:

  1. Your questions are sent directly to our API service
  2. The API processes your question and returns an answer
  3. Both your question and the answer are displayed only on your device
  4. When you close the app or start a new session, previous conversations are not saved

Third-Party Services

Our application uses AWS Bedrock to access Anthropic Claude 3.7 for AI functionality. Your queries are processed through this service but are not stored or retained. The connection to this service is made directly from your device, and we do not maintain any logs or records of these interactions.

Device Permissions

Our app requires minimal permissions to function:

  • Internet access: Required to send queries to our AI service

We do not request access to:

  • Your contacts
  • Your location
  • Your photos or files
  • Your microphone or camera
  • Any other sensitive device features

Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect or solicit data from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at:

  • Email: privacy@gelagleai.com
  • Address: GelagleAI Headquarters

Your Rights

Even though we don't collect your data, you have rights regarding your privacy. You can:

  • Contact us with questions about our privacy practices
  • Report any concerns about our privacy policy or practices
  • Request information about how your queries are processed
